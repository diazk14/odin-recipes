Chorizo Burger

The chorizo burger combines tex mex flavors together in order to create the ultimate burger you can resist contaning an angus beef patty combined with chorizo, hickory bacon with caramelized onions.

This recipe has been requested for a long time, its a family secret, but the time is now! Make your own and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Angus beef patty
  • Hickory bacon
  • Onions
  • Buns
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix angus patty with a tube of chorizo.
  2. Chop your onions up and cook them on a pan unntil they become caramelized.
  3. Cook your patty on the grill and flip once after 10 minutes.
  4. Put your burger together and enjoy.
