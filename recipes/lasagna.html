Vegan lasagna

The recipe was made with organic ingredients to satisfy those who crave a healthy but hearty meal. Every ingredient is plant based, containing no meat at all. We thrive in pushing boundaries by changing the identity of such a classic dish.

The tomatos are farm grown with no pesticides. The lasagne is hand made giving it a unique touch, applying techniques passed down by our renown chef Gusto.

Ingredients

  • lasagne
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Smash the tomatos
  2. Hand make the lasagne
  3. Lay the lasagne down, creating the first layer of the lasagna
  4. Pour tomatos and cheese on every layer
  5. Keep layering until you feel that its adequate
  6. Put into oven in 350 degrees for one hour
  7. Let lasagna sit for 30 minutes
